# Umbra Refund Policy

## Overview

Umbra provides cloud deployment infrastructure for developers. This document outlines our refund policy for all plans and usage charges.

## Subscription Refunds

### Monthly Plans

We offer a **30-day refund policy** for first-time subscribers who haven't deployed production traffic. This applies to:

- Pro plan ($20/month)
- Team plan ($50/month per seat)

If you've deployed production applications or exceeded 100,000 function invocations, you're not eligible for a full refund but may receive prorated credit.

### Annual Plans

Annual subscriptions receive a prorated refund within the first 30 days. After 30 days, you may convert remaining time to account credits but cash refunds are not available.

## Usage-Based Charges

**Usage charges are non-refundable.** This includes:

- Bandwidth overages
- Function invocations beyond plan limits
- Build minutes
- Edge middleware execution time

Usage is metered in real-time. Review your Usage Dashboard before scaling to production.

## How to Request a Refund

1. Open a support ticket at umbra.dev/support
2. Include your team slug and the billing period in question
3. Describe why you're requesting a refund
4. For eligible refunds, processing takes 5-7 business days

## Free Tier and Credits

- Free tier usage is not refundable (it's free)
- Promotional credits expire and cannot be refunded
- Enterprise committed spend is governed by your contract terms

## Billing Disputes

If you believe you were charged incorrectly:

1. Check your Usage Dashboard for detailed breakdowns
2. Review our billing documentation at docs.umbra.dev/billing
3. Open a billing dispute within 60 days of the charge
4. Provide specific dates, amounts, and why you believe it's incorrect
